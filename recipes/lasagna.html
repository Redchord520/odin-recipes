Lasagna

It was the summer of 1974 and my italian grandmother told me I wasn't going to amount to anything. I looked her in the face and said, 'One day i'm gonna be famous!' Here i sit writing a mediorce food blog that most readers skip right over my life story for the recipe. Well here is the recipe you animals!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3/4 lb. Lean Ground Beef
  • 1/2 cup Minced Onion
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, Crushed
  • 1 (28 Ounce) Can Crushed Tomato
  • 2 (6 Ounce) Cans Tomato Paste
