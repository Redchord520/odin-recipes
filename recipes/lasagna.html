Lasagna

Here i sit writing a mediorce food blog that most readers skip right over my life story for the recipe. Well here is the recipe you animals!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3/4 lb. Lean Ground Beef
  • 1/2 cup Minced Onion
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, Crushed
  • 1 (28 Ounce) Can Crushed Tomato
  • 2 (6 Ounce) Cans Tomato Paste
  • 2 (6.5 Ounce) Cans Canned Tomato Sauce
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 2 Tablespoons White Sugar
  • 1-1/2 Teaspoons Dried Basil Leaves
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Fennel Seeds
  • 1 Teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 1-1/2 Teaspoons of Salt, Divided, or to Taste
  • 1/4 Teapsoon Ground Black Pepper
  • 4 Tablespoons Chopped Fresh Parsley
  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 16 Ounces Ricotta cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • 3/4 Pound Mozzarella Cheese, Sliced
  • 3/4 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions

  1. In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic over medium heat until well browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water.
  3. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons parsley.
  4. Simmer, covered, for about 1-1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  5. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodels, and rinse with cold water.
  6. IN a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, reaming parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  7. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  8. To assemble, spread 1-1/2 cups of meat suace in the bottom of a 9x13 baking disk. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with a 1/3 of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1-1/2 cups of meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinlke with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spary foil with cooking spray, or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
